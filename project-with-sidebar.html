Capilano Courier

I continue to do conceptual illustration for a myriad of articles covering News, local Arts and Culture, and Progressive Adcovacy.

The Capilano Courier is a student-run magazine publication, and a staple in Capilano Universities student art and design community. I have worked with the Capilano Courier since the Fall of 2023, starting as a part-time illustrator. Over the years I worked towards becoming a Contracted Crew illustrator, and now hold the position of Production Manager.
