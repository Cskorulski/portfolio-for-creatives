IMPACT

Over 30% of all food produced in Canada is wasted every year, all while 1 in 5 Canadians are considered food insecure! Food waste is not merely an issue effecting climate change, but one that effects our communities on a day to day basis.

IMPACT is a non-profit organization set on addressing youth food insecurity through upcycling, outreach, and clever marketing.

Providing snacks, meal kits, and culinary education, they seek to expose the underbelly of corporate food waste, reduce shame around food insecurity, and help to provide nutricious snacks and meals for low-income children.

PACKAGING

Using kraft paper derrived from vegetable peal pulp, each individual bag can be broken off; ideal for sharing between friends or saving for later.
