MED BED Brochure

Truth and reconcilliation comes in many forms. One often neglected in discussion is the uplifting of Indigenous ecological phillosophy and knowledge of native plants.

In collaboration with Lily Jones and Bel Lui Med-Bed was born. A seed distribution non-profit that works with Indigenous-run gardens in the Coast Salish region; Educating about traditional medicine, native plants, and ethical foraging.

This seed sample brochure packet was devised to help educate, and advertise seed sales that help to support Indigenous seed libraries.
