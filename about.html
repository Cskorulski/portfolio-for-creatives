  • Hello there, I'm Cameron Skorulski!

    My heart lies in the little things of this world; the veins of leaves, wiggles of worms, pools of dew in liverworts, and sour berries.

    Ohhh you want to know what I do for work? Well, I am a graphic nomad; exploring, learning, and forever changing. Ergo, I explore a broad range of projects. My journey has always been emboldened by a strong foundation in illustration. From comics, visual development, and animation; to brand and product design; to fine art and textiles. If there's an opportunity to try something new, you bet I will be there!

    I am guided by a drive for social justice, environmental advocacy, and the emboldening of whimsy in our visual landscape. My process is certainly not perfect, but it is experimental, thoughtful, and laden with careful observation.

    – Thoughtful breaker – Mindful shaker
